Artist Statement

My work seeks to capture the ephemeral moments that define our connection to the world. Through a blend of vibrant colors and dynamic textures, I aim to evoke a sense of wonder and introspection. Each piece is a journey, an exploration of memory, and a celebration of the beauty found in imperfection.
